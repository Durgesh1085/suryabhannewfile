Suryabhan Singh Kushwaha
(Golu Babu)

Advocate Suryabhan Singh Kushwaha, popularly known as Golu Babu, is a respected figure in the legal community of Uttar Pradesh. Known for his sharp analytical skills, in-depth understanding of law, and unshakable commitment to justice, he has represented hundreds of clients in criminal, civil, family, and property disputes.

With years of courtroom experience, his advocacy is driven by integrity, clarity, and relentless preparation. He is admired for his personal approach and clear communication, ensuring every client feels heard, supported, and empowered.

Whether it's defending the innocent, resolving family conflicts, or tackling land ownership disputes — Advocate Golu Babu stands as a pillar of justice.
